Marker integrity

The system uses a hidden <!-- qid:X --> marker on every clinical question to drive UI chip routing. Initial smoke testing showed the model emitting markers on only 23% of turns under load. After the Phase 2 hardening pass — server-side marker injector + registry alignment — average marker emission climbed to 94% across the 10 multi-turn scenarios (bph-3 is excluded as a single-turn urgent triage with no chip-driving questions). 6 of 11 scenarios sit at ≥96%. The two layers of fallback that previously carried the consultation (text-match and contextual rephrase detection) remain in place as defence in depth.

The remaining marker-less messages across all scenarios fall into three intentional non-registry categories: dynamic clinical-interview follow-ups (e.g. "What kind of work was that?"), question-handler branches (e.g. "Of course — what's on your mind?"), and terminal outcome-flow content with schedule links. None drive UI chip routing, so a marker is not required.

Hardening shipped during Phase 2

  • Server-side marker injector (lib/marker-injector.js) — when the model omits a qid marker, the API route matches the assistant text against the registry and injects the correct marker before returning to the client. Idempotent and conservative — leaves text unchanged when no needle matches.
  • Registry fingerprints — added tamsulosin / flomax / alpha-blocker fingerprints to sg-q3-alpha-blocker (ED) and cyclophosphamide / ifosfamide / cytoxan to risk-q7-chemo (MH) so the model's natural rephrasing still resolves correctly.
  • Registry-prompt alignment — corrected risk-q7-chemo registry text to match the prompt's Ask EXACTLY wording (the parenthetical "sometimes called Cytoxan" was in the registry but the prompt told the model to omit it, breaking needle-match).
  • Patient-friendly visit-summary error copy — the manual endConsultation path now matches the auto-trigger path's wording. Replaced "Error generating SOAP note" with the user-facing "We couldn't prepare your visit summary right now. Our team has a copy on file…"
  • Harness streaming-stability fix — the simulator now waits for the assistant bubble's text to stop growing for 800 ms after the typing dots clear, eliminating false-positive idle detection during multi-paragraph messages.
